Non-Sports Trading Cards From Sports Cards Reserve

The trading card hobby extends far beyond sports. Pokemon, Magic: The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh, Star Wars, Marvel, and countless entertainment properties have produced collectible cards that rival sports cards in both collector passion and market value. Sports Cards Reserve is expanding into the non-sports card market to serve collectors across the full spectrum of the trading card hobby.

Pokemon and Gaming Card Collecting

Pokemon cards have experienced explosive growth, with certain vintage holographic cards selling for hundreds of thousands of dollars. The Pokemon card market delivered a documented 3,261 percent return over twenty years, outperforming nearly every traditional investment vehicle. Gaming cards from franchises like Magic: The Gathering carry their own rich collecting tradition, with Alpha and Beta edition cards commanding premium prices from dedicated players and investors alike.

Entertainment and Pop Culture Cards

From vintage Star Wars cards to modern Marvel releases, entertainment trading cards capture moments from the films, shows, and franchises that define pop culture. These cards appeal to both traditional card collectors and fans of specific properties, creating crossover demand that drives value. Sports Cards Reserve sources non-sports cards with the same attention to authenticity and condition that we apply to our sports card inventory.
