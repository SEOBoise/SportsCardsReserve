The Foundation of Card Collecting

Baseball cards have been collected since the 1880s, making them the oldest and most established segment of the trading card market. Vintage cards from the 1950s and 1960s, including Mickey Mantle, Willie Mays, and Hank Aaron, have delivered some of the strongest long-term appreciation in the entire collectibles market. Modern rookies from Topps, Bowman, and Panini continue this tradition with chrome finishes, numbered parallels, and autographed inserts.
